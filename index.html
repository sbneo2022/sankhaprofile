Dr. Sankha Banerjee

About

Sankha finished his doctoral thesis applying machine learning to non-linear dynamical systems from MIT. He finished his finance degree from the Sloan School of Management at MIT.

During his PhD he focussed on using supercomputing facilities available to MIT researchers, for the first time to simulate a three-dimensional flag and derive its analytical constraints on stability . During his PhD, Sankha published 10 peer review journals in the field of Turbulence incorporating computer graphics and invariant theory influencing multiple references from NASA.
